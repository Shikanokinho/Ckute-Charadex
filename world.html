About World

Do you like characters made of food? Cookie Run style but made of crepes? Then you're in the right place !!

The crepikyutes were born from humans' intense cravings for crepes, resulting in the creation of a distant world filled with sweet, savory, spicy, and other types of foods. These little crepes, born from humans, took on life and the taste that their humans desired.

Your crepe can be any flavor, without restrictions, such as cheese, chocolate, fruit; there are no limits to creativity when it comes to these little crepes.

you can: any type of edible food
